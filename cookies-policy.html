We do not store or track personal identifiers. Any processing handled across storage parameters is established entirely to serve critical interactive functionalities or to safely render analytical tracking structures.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your computer or mobile hardware structure by web pages you choose to run. In addition to standard script-based cookie elements, modern systems utilize local client web storage features (known as localStorage or sessionStorage parameters) inside the browser utility folder to speed up data execution tasks.

2. How We Use Storage Elements

Spin The Wheel Game implements storage files to handle structural state retention. Specifically, we access browser storage mechanisms to:

  • Save your active string inputs or customized lists of names within the textbox so that refreshing the window does not delete your manual structural inputs.
  • Maintain selected configurations, toggle states (sound properties, confetti rules), or visual dashboard interface options.
  • Provide standard statistical signals regarding anonymous traffic routes across the site.

3. Types of Parameters We Use

Essential Session Storage: These are critical local values loaded into individual tabs. They track standard calculations while adjusting mathematical coordinates to verify the accurate placement of segment text lines along the canvas perimeter.

Functional Persistent Storage: Created directly via browser-managed storage elements to securely lock customized canvas setups across multi-day operations without asking you to log into remote network profiles.

Analytical Tracking & Ads: Managed alongside third-party service hooks. These snippets process structural log properties to monitor device resolutions, regional distributions, and referral parameters. This tracking helps optimize performance trends without compromising identity metrics.

4. Managing Your Preferences

Most desktop and mobile browsers allow you to modify cookie handling rules via their integrated settings menus. You can set rules to decline new tracker components, drop existing logs immediately on program termination, or completely disable client-side data cache allocations.

Please Note: If you choose to configure your local browser to clear all caching parameters entirely, you will need to re-enter your favorite lists of names or customized spin settings every time you visit Spin The Wheel Game.
